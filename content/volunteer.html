You can impact our community, learn skills, and meet new people while volunteering. What would you like to do?

  • Construction:  Help build a Habitat home any Tuesday, Thursday, or Saturday

  • ReStores: Help to sort donations, stock shelves, or work the register at our home goods and building materials resale store

  • Committees/Office: Help with office tasks and mailings, special events, or educational programs and family mentoring.

  • Youth Volunteers: Help with plantings on a new home or yard clean-up on an older house. Please note: All volunteers on an active construction site must be at least 18 years old. Occasional opportunities for younger participants, 12-17, will be listed here from time to time.
