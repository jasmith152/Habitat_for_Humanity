Volunteers needed for Stone Crab Jam

Habitat for Humanity of Citrus County seeks Habitat volunteers to assist with this year's Stone Crab Jam sponsored through Crystal River Rotary Club on November 3, in Crystal River.

Every year, King's Bay Rotary Club accesses 200 volunteers from a variety of Citrus County organizations to run the wine and beer booths, work the ticket gates, trash removal, table maintenance and breakdown. Two 4-hour shifts are available. They are from 3:00 p.m. to 7:30 p.m., and from 7:30 p.m. to 11:00 p.m.

Volunteers are asked to check in one half hour prior to their shift. Check-in is at the Seminol Club on 135 NE 3rd Street in Crystal River. Park close to the Walgreens and wall over to the Seminol Club.

Once checked-in, you will receive a worker badge and wrist band. Habitat Executive Director Terry Steele will assign chores.

For more information, contact CD Perez at (352) 601-6582.

Calling All Golfers

(Crystal River, Sept. 8, 2012) Ominous skies and pelting rain were no match for the bright smile of Adam Fuchs as he anxiously opened the front door his new Habitat home located on 8816 North Delene Terrace in Crystal River.
