By donating a car to Habitat’s Cars for Homes™ car donation program, you help us build houses in partnership with families in need of decent, affordable shelter.
It is quick and easy to donate a car, truck, boat, RV, motorcycle, construction equipment or other vehicle — running or not! Plus, when you donate your car, your vehicle donation may be eligible for a tax deduction- if you itemize. (Consult the IRS or your tax adviser for details.)
