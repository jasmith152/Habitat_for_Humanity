HFHCC builds frame homes on concrete foundations. The typical house has approximately 1,173 square feet of furnished living area, set on varying size lots in quiet neighborhoods. Most houses have three bedrooms with 1 3/4 baths, a covered front entry and paved driveway, but no garage or carport. A kitchen stove and refrigerator are supplied by the Whirlpool Corporation for every Habitat home.
