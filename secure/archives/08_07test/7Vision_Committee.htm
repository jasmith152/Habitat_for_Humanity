Vision Ad-Hoc Committee Report
July 2003

 

The Vision Committee has met twice since the last board meeting. Gil has recruited a couple of people from the community who will be genuine assets to our team. The committee has grown to a working size of 6 members, and will report directly to John Thrumston.

 

The members of the committee consist of:

Gil Brier – Chairperson

Bonnie Peterson – Vice Chair

Mark Whittaker

Otis Long

Chris Evans (Sergeant at the CC Sheriff’s office)

Judith Tear (Emergency Planner at CC Heath Department)

 

We have set up and agreed to a schedule of meetings that we expect will allow us to deliver a five-year plan for this affiliate by no later than Thanksgiving of 2003.

 

Submitted for the Vision Committee

 

 

Bonnie Peterson
