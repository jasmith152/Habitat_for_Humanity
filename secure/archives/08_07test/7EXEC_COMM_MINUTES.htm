EXECUTIVE BOARD MEETING

HABITAT FOR HUMANITY OF CITRUS COUNTY, INC.

MINUTES JULY 15, 2003  3:30 pm

 

 

 

The meeting was called to order by Norm Peterson, President.  Present also were John Thrumston, Linda Deptola, Lorna Nimmo , ED  Michelle Maidlow  and Dan Groner.

 

The first matter involved information learned from the Habitat International website.  Since two members of the same family may not serve on the Executive Board it has been suggested that the following changes take place to the slate of officers:

                        Linda Deptola will serve as Secretary

                        Bonnie Peterson will serve as Recording Secretary

 

Many Corporations have more than one Secretary position.  Linda will take minutes at the Executive BOD meetings and Bonnie will take minutes at the general meetings.

 

MOTION by John Thrumston to accept job description for Secretary positions, seconded by Dan Groner, all approved.

 

Finance report was discussed.  Michelle has transferred records to Quick Books and we will eventually use the Habitrax system provided by International.  Several questions were asked as to where payments on mortgages show and what the beginning balances for escrow accounts indicate.  Answers were given by both Norm and Michelle.  Mr. Peterson passed out explanation of escrow accounts.  Many are under funded and Mr. Peterson will meet with partners to explain situation and resolve.  The solution choice will be, sub loan to be paid off monthly, refinance of the entire mortgage, or they may pay in full to fund the deficit.  Some of the problem has been the huge increase in insurance costs.  It was suggested that during the first year most of the payment should go to escrow much as a conventional mortgage.  That way we would not be in a position of paying insurance and taxes for partners and having them pay us.  Collett mortgage was paid off and proceeds show in the report.

 

Mr. Peterson expressed a need for the Executive Board to understand the Finance Report so as to be able to answer questions at the general board meeting.  We have also reached the level at which an Audit is required by Habitat International.  This will be scheduled for February and will be in next year’s budget.

 

Vision Committee was discussed.  Gill Brier is Chairperson and Bonnie Peterson is Vice-Chair.  They will be meeting two to three times per month.  Chairpersons will be approved at next general board meeting.

 

Volunteer Appreciation was discussed.  There will definitely be a picnic for volunteers in the fall.  Awards will be planned such as “golden hammer”.  This will be held annually.

 

Committee diversity was discussed.  It is important to have members of many different occupations and nationalities on the Board.  Members from the Sheriffs Department, media and different ethnic groups were discussed.

 

The Executive Board will consider having quarterly meetings open to the public at strategic location through out Citrus County in an effort to educate the public on our affiliate and the opportunities for volunteers.

 

Other committee reports were discussed.  By MOTION of Linda Deptola, seconded by Dan Groner the approval of Candice King as a partner was deselected.

 

John Thrumston questioned the need for tee shirts to make available to volunteers.  At this time we could only sell shirts, not give them away.

 

Behuniak family was discussed.  They are now 90 days behind on mortgage.  A plan will be presented by Mr. Peterson to bring them current.  In the future they will not be allowed to be late even one day.  The Behuniaks have been made aware of all county facilities that could help them.  This entire situation will be addressed by a letter from our attorney as it is as important to manage this situation properly as it is to collect the money owed.

 

There being no further business to bring before the Executive Board a MOTION was made by Dan Groner to adjourn, seconded and approved.

 

 

Submitted by:

 

 

Linda J. Deptola, Secretary

Habitat for Humanity Citrus County
