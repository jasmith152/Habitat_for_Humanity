Home Store Report

July Board of Directors Meeting

22 July 2003

We have completed ten weeks of operation in the everything for The Home Store. Following is a summary of the sales and cash generated through the first ten weeks:

Gross Sales: $19,388.28

Average Weekly Sales: $ 1,938.82

Average Daily Sales: $ 395.67

Sales, net of sales tax: $18,290.83

Cash generated: $19,416.03 ($19,216.03 deposited in bank; $200 to fund till).

Fiscal Year 2003-2004 got off to a slow start the first two weeks of July, but picked up last week. Sales through the first three weeks of the current FY were $4,910.63, or a weekly average of $1,636.87 and a daily average of $350.75.

Donation intake continues well. Challenges going forward continue to be to build sales and volunteer recruitment. Volunteer crew for the truck pick up activity (healthy males) is a continuing focus, as one of the primary crew is travelling for the Summer and the other has cut back to one day per week due to health concerns. Board input or volunteer referral would be greatly appreciated.

Respectfully submitted,

John C. Tuffy
