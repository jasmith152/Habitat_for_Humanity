HABITAT FOR HUMANITY OF CITRUS COUNTY

BOARD OF DIRECTORS MEETING

JUNE 26, 2003   5:15 p m

 

 

The meeting was called to order by Barbara Rezac, President.  Present at the meeting in addition to Barbara Rezac were Rosann Strawn, Michael Crusco, Terry McMillan, Mark Whittaker, Russ VanDyke, Bonnie Peterson, John Thrumston, Gil Brier, Dan Groner and Linda Deptola, all Directors.   Guest included Norm Peterson, John Tuffy, Otis Long, Dolly Dugan, Dick Derosch and Michelle Maidlow.  Lorna Nimmo, ED was present.

A quorum was established.

 

A prayer was offered by Mark Whittaker.

 

The first order of business was the approval of the minutes of the last meeting.  The only corrections were to the spelling of names.  Gill Brier made a MOTION to approve the minutes, seconded by Dan Groner, all approved.

 

The Treasurer’s Report was presented.  Carl Fairbanks was not present so Barbara Rezac presented the information.  There were several questions regarding miscellaneous income and the Home Store. A review of mortgage payments was presented; two are delinquent for the month. Not all questions could be answered due to the absence of Mr. Fairbanks but by MOTION of Gil Brier the report was accepted, seconded by Mike Crusco, all approved.

 

It was requested to close the Raymond James account and transfer funds to Am South.  This was accomplished by MOTION of Terry McMillan, seconded by John Thrumston, all approved.

 

UNFINISHED BUSINESS

 

By laws are still being worked on.  Nothing to report at this time.

 

Strategic Planning:  Gil states that the committee needs a chairperson. It was decided that this will be addressed by the new Board.

 

Tee Shirts are available for those who want them. 

 

COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Building Committee: Home is being designed to accommodate handicap partner.  This should not be an expensive change.  A “punch list” is needed for Spencer.

 

Family Services:  There are two partners approaching 200 house of sweat equity.  Discussion of home will begin soon.  Twenty six families came to the most recent orientation.

 

Site Selection:  No questions.

 

Executive Director Report:  Lorna began discussion of Elaine Coursen’s water problem.  Executive Board decided to contact an independent lab to do testing.  Their report is that well must be “shocked” to destroy iron bacteria.  May need iron filter.  Shock treatment is first step. Could be ongoing problem.  New Board will monitor.

 

Home Store Report:  After six weeks of operation gross sales are $12,329.51.  Grand opening was a big success.  Saturday, June 21st was our first $1,000 day.

 

Affiliate will join the Builders Association.  It has been commented that the store is clean and the public enjoys shopping there.  Dan Groner suggested that we donate TVs to CASA.

 

It was pointed out that the Board does not “run the store”.  Any questions or suggestions should go to Lorna Nimmo and on to John.  Must keep communication in line.

 

Lorna Nimmo, our new ED gave a brief history of her experience and described her background for the Board.

 

NEW BUSINESS:

 

Nomination Committee presented slate of Directors for election.  A MOTION was made by Terry McMillan to add Michelle Maidlow to the slate.  She is interested in the Treasurer position.  Motion seconded by Russ VanDyke, all approved.

 

The slate of nominees was presented in ballot form but it was pointed out if all were in agreement as to the acceptance of the slate that the Board could direct the Secretary to cast the votes.  This being said Russ VanDyke made a MOTION that the Secretary cast the votes of the membership, seconded by John Thrumston, all approved.

 

The duly elected Board of Directors for the years 2003 to 2004 are as follows:  Doug Vollmer, Mike Crusco, Rosann Strawn, Dan Groner, Bonnie Peterson, Linda Deptola, Mark Whittaker, Gil Brier, Terry McMillan, John Thrumston, Gene Rehmann, Norm Peterson, Dolly Dugan, Otis Long, Darrel Reneau and Michelle Maidlow.

 

There being no further business to bring before this Board the meeting was adjourned by MOTION of Mike Crusco, seconded by Gil Brier, all approved.  There will be a meeting immediately convened for the election of officers.

 

Submitted by :

 

 

Linda J. Deptola

Secretary

BOARD OF DIRECTORS MEETING

JUNE 26, 2003     6:30 P M

 

Immediately following the regular Board meeting a meeting was called to order for the purpose of electing offices for the year 2003 to 2004.  Lorna Nimmo performed the installation of Directors.

 

Each board member seeking election as an officer spoke to the group briefly. A ballot was passed out with the names of those seeking each office.  At this time a MOTION was made to nominate Michelle Maidlow to the position of Treasurer and add her name to the ballot.  MOTION by John Thrumston, seconded and approved.

 

The ballots were marked and collected by the tellers, Judy Groner and Dick Deroach.  The counting of ballots took place immediately.  The following Officers were elected by the Board for 2003-2004:

 

                        President     Norm Peterson

                        Vice-President     John Thrumston

                        Treasurer    Michelle Maidlow

                        Secretary    Bonnie Peterson

 

Lorna Nimmo performed the installation of each officer individually

 

A parting gift of thanks was presented to Barbara Rezac.  Her name was placed on the plaque which records presidents of the Board.

 

The new President presented sheets representing the various committees that should or are formed.  The President exercised his right to appoint chairpersons.  Those chairpersons are:    

 

A MOTION was made by Mike Crusco to set the number of Board member for the year at sixteen (16).  The motion was seconded and approved.

 

Linda Deptola volunteered to serve as Chairperson for Family Services; Gene Rehmann was suggested for Church Relations Chairperson.

 

ACTION ITEMS AS FOLLOWS:
